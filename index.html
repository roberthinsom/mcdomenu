McDonald’s Menu Philippines Highlights: What’s New and What’s Classic

1. Classic Burgers and Sandwiches

McDonald’s classic burgers are a staple for any visitor. Here’s a look at the must-try options on the McDonald’s Menu Philippines in 2024:

  • Big Mac – The iconic Big Mac remains a top choice, featuring two beef patties, lettuce, cheese, pickles, and the special Big Mac sauce on a sesame seed bun. Priced at ₱165, it’s a reliable choice for those craving a classic taste.
  • Quarter Pounder with Cheese – For a meatier option, the Quarter Pounder with Cheese, priced at ₱190, offers a larger beef patty topped with cheese, onions, pickles, and mustard.
  • McChicken – A fan favorite, the McChicken is priced at ₱145 and comes with a crispy chicken patty, lettuce, and mayonnaise, sandwiched between a soft bun.
  • Fillet-O-Fish – At ₱160, this sandwich features a breaded fish fillet with tartar sauce and lettuce, perfect for those looking for a lighter alternative to beef or chicken.

2. New Additions and Limited-Time Offers

McDonald’s always brings innovation to its menu, and 2024 is no exception. Here are some of the newest items making waves on the McDonald’s Menu Philippines:

  • Spicy McChicken Deluxe – For those who like a kick, the Spicy McChicken Deluxe is a recent addition. Priced at ₱175, it features a spicier, crispy chicken patty, complemented by a special spicy sauce and jalapeño peppers.
  • Double Quarter Pounder – A new take on a classic, the Double Quarter Pounder comes with two quarter-pound beef patties and is priced at ₱245. It’s perfect for those with a hearty appetite.
  • McFlurry with Brownie Bits – This new McFlurry flavor, priced at ₱130, includes rich brownie pieces mixed into vanilla soft serve, providing a delightful twist on the classic McFlurry.
  • Cheesy McBites – These are small, cheese-filled treats perfect for snacking. Available for ₱85, they are served warm with a gooey cheese center.

3. Breakfast Favorites

The McDonald’s Menu Philippines is renowned for its breakfast options. In 2024, here are the top breakfast items to start your day right:

  • Breakfast Chicken McMuffin – For a quick and satisfying breakfast, the Breakfast Chicken McMuffin, priced at ₱150, offers a grilled chicken patty with egg and cheese in a soft English muffin.
  • Pancakes and Sausages – Priced at ₱155, this breakfast classic includes fluffy pancakes served with sausage links and a side of syrup.
